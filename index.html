Bespoke Interiors

Cubics Design & Décor is a leading interior design and build company in Dubai offering aesthetic Design & Turnkey solutions for clients spread across banking, corporate, retail, hospitality, residential and various other sectors. Our focus lies in creating immaculate interiors that are sophisticated, refined and reflective of our client’s vision and personality. The highly experienced team steadfastly strives to design and deliver solutions that exceed expectations, which makes us one of the most sought-after Interior & Turnkey service providers in the UAE.

Sustainability and Well-being

Sustainable office design improves the performance of employees because people think, feel and work better in offices that have more natural lighting, cleaner indoor air quality, comfortable temperature, inspiring décor with eco-friendly materials and biophilia. A healthier office environment, therefore, improves staff morale, employee retention, productivity, and profitability. Our design philosophy is crafted around responsible, sustainable and reliable design and development practices.

Smart Office & Digital Solutions

We work with our channel partners in harnessing technology and using data to enhance the workplaces of the future. Standalone technology will be replaced by integrated solutions that connect building facilities with the occupier’s premises. Our IoT (Internet of Things) & AI powered solutions enable workplaces and facilities to function more intelligently, more efficiently and more effectively. Temperature, Carbon di oxide (CO2), Humidity can all be measured and regulated using IOT technology. Air-conditioning and lighting are operated remotely and can be adjusted according to the time of day and, tasks and schedules can be automated.
