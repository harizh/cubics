ABOUT US

Cubics Design & Décor is a high-end Interior Design & Fit-Out Company located in Dubai that has carved a niche for itself in the field of design and fit-out. It has been more than a decade now since Cubics has been offering quality Turnkey solutions and Consultancy services to a diverse range of clients. Cubics has added its special touch to Banks, Corporates, Executives, Exchanges, Travels, Restaurants, Showrooms, Retail Stores and Furnished Apartments in the Middle East and Africa.
